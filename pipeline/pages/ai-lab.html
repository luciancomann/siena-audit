curated sessions

What is AI Lab

AI lab brings together founders, executives, and operators from leading brands to talk about what's working in AI. Each session features a brand that's gone beyond the hype—Brooklinen, Magic Spoon, Coterie—and the people running their operations.

How it works

Curated panels

Intimate discussions. Featured brand shares their AI journey, then open conversation with the group.

lunches and dinners

Roundtables

Smaller gatherings over food and drinks. Longer format. Deeper into specific challenges and solutions.

published insights

Published insights

We record, document, and share key takeaways. Available to the broader community.

why attend?

Become an AI practitioner

AI lab exists because the best insights come from people doing the work, not selling tools.

01.

Real Metrics

Move beyond vanity numbers. See how merchants measure AI's impact on conversion, retention, and customer lifetime value. Frameworks you can use immediately.

02.

Your Peers

Connect with operators facing the same challenges. Build relationships with people who understand the complexity of scaling AI in live commerce environments.

03.

AI Ecosystem

From autonomous support to business intelligence, explore how AI touches every part of the customer journey. Integration strategies that compound over time.

04.

First Access

Early exposure to emerging tools and methods. Shape the conversation before trends become common knowledge. Stay ahead of market shifts without the overwhelm.

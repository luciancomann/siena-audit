Our mission is to humanize the internet by turning customer interactions into meaningful relationships.

How it all started.

Envisioning a new way of doing customer experience



We spent over a decade as Shopify merchants scaling ecommerce brands. We experienced first-hand how customer support tools often fell short—automation without real solutions, personalization, or insights. That's why we built Siena.



Siena joins your team by integrating seamlessly with your help desk and commerce stack. It provides fully autonomous, end-to-end customer resolutions while staying true to your brand DNA.



At Siena’s core is our agentic brand intelligence layer that captures and understands customer interactions deeply, enabling deep personalization and actionable business insights. Siena continuously learns from your best interactions, knowledge sources, guidance, and team expertise, becoming smarter every day.

Today, Siena powers hundreds of millions of conversations for brands and handles up to 80% of interactions while maintaining an average CSAT of 4.81.



Our mission is to humanize the internet by turning customer interactions into meaningful relationships. Siena is your partner for commerce growth, exceptional CX, and lasting brand loyalty.
